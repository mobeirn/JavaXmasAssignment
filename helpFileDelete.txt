---------------------------- Delete Words Help ---------------------------
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Delete all instances of a particular word
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
To delete all instances of a particular word, type in 2 from the Main menu,
then type 3 in the Search menu, then type 1 in the Delete Words menu.
Type in the word you wish to delete when asked.
You will be asked for confirmation before the word is deleted.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Delete a word at a particular index
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
To delete a word at a particular index, type in 2 from the Main menu,
then type 3 in the Search menu, then type 2 in the Delete Words menu.
Type in the index of word you wish to delete when asked.
You will be asked for confirmation before the word is deleted.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
