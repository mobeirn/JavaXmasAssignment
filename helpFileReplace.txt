--------------------------- Replace Words Help ---------------------------
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Replace all instances of a particular word
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
To replace all instances of a particular word, type in 2 from the Main menu,
then type 2 in the Search menu, then type 1 in the Replace Words menu.
Type in the word you are looking for when asked.
Type in the replacement word when asked.
You will be asked for confirmation before the word is replaced.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Replace a word at a particular index
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
To replace a word at a particular index, type in 2 from the Main menu,
then type 2 in the Search menu, then type 2 in the Replace Words menu.
Type in the index of word you are looking for when asked.
Type in the replacement word when asked.
You will be asked for confirmation before the word is replaced.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
